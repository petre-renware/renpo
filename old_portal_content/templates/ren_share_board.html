  • It's true that somebody else can read my mail?

    Regarding the company mail, yes it is. But nobody has time to do that, and nobody it is paid for. The mail system is protected with a Message Content Protection (MCP) component for more reasons:

    • to protect the company against outbounding mails with confidential information (for example technical / design documents, commercial or legal non-public information about company customers, and so on)
    • to protect aginst outbounding mails with different kind of sensitive information (for example valid redit card numbers)
    • to protect aginst outbounding mails with different kind of aggressive words
    • to protect aginst outbounding mails with spam content in case of an attacker haked our mail servers
    • to protect aginst outbounding mails with potential legal implications

    Anyway, the mail system will retire for a while such messages, but will send them. If situation is a repeating one, a notification mail will be sent to more stuff persons. In such cases is possible to read the content of that mails, BUT FOR SURE YOU'LL BE INFORMED AND ASKED about this situation, because it is likely possible to know nothing about it.

    Otherwise, we recommend you to not use the company mail for personal sensitive problems. First, the company mail address which was given to you, it is still under company responsibility simply becase the mail server is registered in DNS and CA public systems as being the company property. Second, regardless the potential legal issues, we do not like to to get into the situation to be listed on different black lists which can BLOCK ALL MAILS until the issues are solved... And third reason is related to ethics and moraliy and we do not think is the case for much more details.

    Your personal problems are yours, keep them for you, and if we can help you, for sure we'll do it! But without creating 'social cases' inside company. Ask for help but do it with dignity and honor not with publicity!
