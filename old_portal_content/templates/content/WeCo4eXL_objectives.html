This collection of WeCo4eXL systems offer you a way to define and construct a VISION about your company WELLNESS.
How will be money invest and what bennefits and yields wil you obtain from this investments; these are the main aspects that will be highlighted by this system:
  • How much you need to invest
  • How will be money used
  • When to invest
  • What will be your yields
GENERAL MONEY FLOW. The system will follow the general money out-flow and will collect all necessary data from accounting to construct the picture of money transformation from EXPENSES to INVESTMENTS to GOODS and finally to BENEFITS and YIELDS.

WeCo4eXL is a package containg the following products:
  • CFWeCo4eXL – Cash Flow Control
  • BuWeCo4eXL – Budget Control
  • CoWeCo4eXL – Cost Control
  • AIWeCo4eXL – Assets & Investments Control
