By using the RENPMS system allows a project manager to answer to critical questions of stakeholders regarding:
  • Schedule, Deadlines & Time Framing – project completion in agreed & assumed timeframe
  • Budget & Costs Framing – project completion in projected financial terms
  • Potential Risks – what are the risk we are exposing to and how can be avoided
  • Cash Flow – manage payments as when and how much, debt deadlines & funding requirements
  • Quality Conformity – bounding to technical, environmental & legal requirements
  • Various other administrative issues like missing documents, closed documents, meetings
  • Security & audit features – trace of who and when modifyied records (not in SL Edition); full log of all system operations
  • The process of periodically project review and ‘find out the real situation’
Please refer to product user documentation or sales presentation for more details.
